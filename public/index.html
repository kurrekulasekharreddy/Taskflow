Help & FAQ

How do I create a task?

Click the + button on the Tasks page or use the Quick Add button on the Dashboard.

How do I organize tasks?

Use categories to group related tasks. You can create custom categories with colors and icons.

Can I set due dates?

Yes! When creating or editing a task, set the due date field. Tasks will appear on the calendar.

How do notes work?

Notes are quick text snippets. Pin important notes to keep them at the top.

Can I export my data?

Go to Settings and click Export Data to download all your tasks and notes.
